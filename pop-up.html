Data Visualization

To read my data portrait you simply need to know that the devices depicted are the devices they represent: top left is a monitor, bottom left is a phone, top middle is a laptop, bottom middle is a tablet, and right is an iPad. When a screen is lit up it represents time that the device was used in hours. The calendar at the top shows what day of data collection is currently being represented. The color of the screen when it is lit up represents what I did with the device during that moment.
To track my data I created a spreadsheet with hours of the day as the x-axis and the type of device on the y-axis. If I used a device within an hour period I would put a circle in that cell as well as the abbreviation for the category of usage.
